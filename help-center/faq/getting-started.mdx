## Overview

To start with your Master in Public Strategy courses:

1. Log in to your student dashboard.
2. Navigate to "My Courses".
3. Click on a course to view materials.

## Accessing Course Content

- Video lectures and readings are listed per module.
- Download assignments from the "Tasks" tab.
- Submit work using the "Upload" button before the deadline.

## Support

If you don't see your courses, contact support at support@publicstrategy.edu.
