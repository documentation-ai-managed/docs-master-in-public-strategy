---
title: How to Enroll in a Course
description: Step-by-step instructions for enrolling in a course in Master in Public Strategy.
---

## Overview

Enroll in new courses directly from your dashboard.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Dashboard">
    ````
    Visit https://dashboard.publicstrategy.edu and enter your credentials.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Available Courses">
    ````
    Click on "Courses" in the main menu to view all options.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Enroll'">
    ````
    Find your chosen course and press the "Enroll" button.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Registration">
    ````
    You will see a confirmation message and the course will appear in your "My Courses" section.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
