---
title: Can't Log In to Student Dashboard
description: How to resolve login problems for Master in Public Strategy.
---

## Symptoms

- Error: "Invalid credentials"
- Login page reloads with no feedback

## Causes

- Incorrect email or password
- Account not activated

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the emailed link.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check account activation">
    ````
    Find the activation email in your inbox. Click the confirmation link.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Use your browser settings to clear cache and try again.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
